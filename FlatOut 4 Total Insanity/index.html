About This Game

FlatOut 4: Total Insanity introduces an array of exciting new features, including formidable vehicles, diverse gaming modes, and thrilling tracks. Among its offerings are two exclusive arenas: the Factory Arena and the Ice Lake Arena. Additionally, the game boasts two exclusive drivers, Dave Worker and Gadget Stewart. Two new vehicles, the Repair Truck and Ice Breaker, join the ranks, adding to the game's vehicular variety. Customization is key in FlatOut 4: Total Insanity, with a wide range of highly customizable cars at your disposal. Players can unlock a total of 27 distinct vehicles, each with its unique attributes and design options. The game offers an engaging Arena Mode, featuring three different play styles: Survivor, Capture the Flag, and Death Match. For those seeking a competitive thrill, the Hot Seat Mode allows you to take turns with friends and family in a spirited competition of stunts and skill.
