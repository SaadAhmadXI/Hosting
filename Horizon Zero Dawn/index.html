About This Game

Horizon Zero Dawn is the game of the Horizon game series. The plot follows Aloy, a hunter in a world overrun by machines, who sets out to uncover her past. The player uses ranged weapons, a spear, and stealth to combat mechanical creatures and other enemy forces. A skill tree in the skill menu provides the player with abilities and bonuses. Player can explore the world to discover new locations and take on side quests. Horizon Zero Dawn is Guerrilla Games' first property since Killzone in 2004 and its role-playing video game. Development began in 2011 after the completion of Killzone 3, with director Mathijs de Jonge considering it the riskiest idea pitched at time.
