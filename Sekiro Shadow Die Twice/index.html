About This Game

In the realm of 'Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice,' you assume the role of the 'one-armed wolf,' a warrior marked by disgrace and disfigurement but given a second chance at life. Your solemn duty is to protect a young lord, the last in an ancient bloodline, from the clutches of formidable adversaries, including the formidable Ashina clan. As the young lord falls into captivity, you embark on an unwavering quest to reclaim your lost honor, even if it means facing death itself. Released in 2019, 'Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice' is an action-adventure game masterfully crafted by FromSoftware. The game offers a gripping and challenging experience, immersing players in a richly detailed world and a narrative that explores themes of honor, loyalty, and redemption.
