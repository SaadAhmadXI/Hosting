Setup Size = 32.8 GB   Version : Deluxe Edition

Platforms: PlayStations, Windows, and Xbox

About This Game

Experience the epic clash of histories mightiest warriors in For Honor an action-packed game developed and published by Ubisoft. Step into the boots of legendary fighters such as knights, samurai, and vikings, each with their distinct combat styles and rich heritage. Controlled from a third-person perspective For Honor offers a visceral and Immersive gameplay experience that transports you to the heart of the battlefield. Engage in intense melee combat, master the art of war, and prove your mettle in this thrilling historical showdown. Prepare for honor, glory, and unyielding combat in For Honor!
