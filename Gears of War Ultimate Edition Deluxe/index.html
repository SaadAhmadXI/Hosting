The narrative of Gears of War thrusts players into the heart of an intense and perilous struggle for survival against the Locust Horde a nightmarish race that emerges from the depths of the planet. In the Ultimate Edition, the gripping story unfolds with enhanced visuals that breathe new life into the harrowing tale.
